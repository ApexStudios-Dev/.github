Library Mod required by all of ApexStudios' mods



See here for more in-depth details...

 

 

This is a library mod for MinecraftForge required by all of ApexStudios' mods, such as Fantasy's Furniture, Fantasy's Dice, Infused Foods etc. You download this mod on either Modrinth or CurseForge or even view the source on GitHub.

 

Note: Starting from Minecraft 1.19, this mod comes self-contained within our mod jars, and this means you do not have to install this alongside our mods
